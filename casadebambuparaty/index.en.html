Casa de Bambu

Hosting for couples and groups from 5 to 10 pax.

We are a family from Paraty who loves to receive guests! Come visit the area and stay at our Bambu House.

We offer a very charming environment along with great local travel tips! In the house there are 2 identical toilets and an external area with hammocks facing a garden filled with original Mata Atlântica species.

You will have a fully equipped kitchen available to cook your meals or just a quick snack (stove, fridge, microwaves, blender, toaster and utensils). Every hosting option offers breakfast and room cleaning service.

You're very welcome!
